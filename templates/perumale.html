About MedCare

MedCare is an AI-powered diagnostic helper that aims to make preliminary health assessments accessible, instant, and private. Under the hood, the system uses a local Random Forest Classifier trained on clinical data mapping combinations of 130+ symptoms to corresponding medical prognoses.

We leverage client-side execution parameters combined with machine learning models to supply immediate confidence scores and risk factor breakdowns.

🔒 Privacy-First Storage Commitment
Your personal details and selected symptoms are processed strictly in-memory during the query cycle. We do not store your medical data or diagnostic history in any database or disk log. Only general application feedback is stored in our database, keeping your health assessments 100% anonymous and private.
